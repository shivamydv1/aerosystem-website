FAQ's

Find quick answers to common questions.

01
What is the certified payload capacity of the Euler Turbo EV?

The Euler Turbo EV has a certified payload capacity of 1 tonne, making it ideal for various commercial applications.

02
Is the Euler Turbo EV suitable for seamless cargo delivery within and across cities?

Yes, the Turbo EV is specifically designed for intra-city and inter-city cargo delivery with its 180+ km range and fast charging capabilities.

03
What is the certified and real-world driving range of the Euler Turbo EV?

The certified range is 180+ km, and the real-world range varies between 150-180 km depending on load, terrain, and driving conditions.

04
How comfortable is the Euler Turbo EV for drivers managing long delivery hours?

The Turbo EV 1000 is designed with a spacious cabin, ergonomic seating, and electronic power steering to ensure maximum comfort. Its optimized suspension system with 3/8-leaf springs offers a smooth ride, reducing driver fatigue during extended work hours.

05
How long does it take to fully charge the Euler Turbo EV, and what charging options are available?

A full charge takes approximately 5-6 hours with a standard charger, while fast charging can provide 50 km range in just 15 minutes.
